Get Started!

We are your quick and convenient access to a licensed doctor.

We can assess, diagnose and treat many common medical problems without you having to visit a busy walk-in clinic or emergency department.

Avoid having to travel or be exposed to other people’s illnesses. Your prescription can even be sent to your regular pharmacist who may even deliver to your front door.

Quick and convenient from start to finish!

What we can do:

  • clickMD™ can allow you to connect with a fully licensed doctor in a timely and convenient manner.
  • Your doctor will be able to assess and diagnose many common medical problems without you having to visit a busy walk-in clinic or emergency department.
  • If you are prescribed medication by one of our doctors, we can even forward it to your pharmacist for your convenience.
  • We can provide services to residents of Ontario, who are in Ontario at the time of assessment with valid OHIP coverage.
  • Unlike going to regular walk-in clinics, your family doctor will not be penalized if you use clickMD™ through our on-demand service platforms.

What we cannot do:

  • We cannot see patients who are outside of Ontario at the time of assessment.
  • Although patients require OHIP coverage to use clickMD™ on the internet or through our mobile applications, we cannot bill OHIP for our services for our on-demand services.
  • We are unable to provide insurance or disability application forms, and we won't be able to refer you to other health care providers.
  • Our doctors cannot prescribe narcotics, benzodiazepines or other restricted substances. They will also not be able to provide diagnoses for certain complex medical conditions.
  • Blood tests, urine tests, x-rays and any other radiologic investigations cannot be done using this service.
Remember: if you need urgent medical care, please go to your nearest emergency department or call 9-1-1!

Who Are We?

  • clickMD™ is an online video conferencing service that connects patients with doctors in a timely and convenient manner.

clickMD™ uses a secure video conferencing platform that is PHIPA (Personal Health Information Protection Act) compliant. All medical information is stored in an approved and secure electronic medical record system only. No personal information will be shared. Medical information will only be accessed as part of the provision of medical services to patients and/or their legal guardians.
